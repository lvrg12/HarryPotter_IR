This
book was art
directed by David Saylor. The
art for both the jacket and the interior was
created using pastels on toned printmaking paper. The
text was set in 12-point Adobe Garamond, a typeface based on
the sixteenth-century type designs of Claude Garamond, redrawn by
Robert Slimbach in 1989. The book was typeset by Brad Walrod
and was printed and bound at Quebecor World in Fairfield,
Pennsylvania. The Managing Editor was Manuela
Soares and the Manufacturing
Director was Angela
Biola.
